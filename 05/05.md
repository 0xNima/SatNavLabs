### b) Observation file

**i. GPS satellite identification - PRNs in the first epoch**

`G<PRN>` are GPS satellites: G01, G04, G28, G32, G31, G19, G17, G03, G09, G06, G02

**ii. Observation interval (GPS time / UTC)**

- Interval: 1 second
- First observation - GPS time: `2026-06-13 13:00:00`
- First observation - UTC = GPS − leap seconds (18 s) = `2026-06-13 12:59:42`

**iii. Location and units of the four measurement types (GPS L1)**

- Records present: C1C, L1C, S1C, C1X, L1X, S1X (no Doppler for GPS)
- Units:
  - Phase: full cycles
  - Pseudorange: meters
  - Doppler: Hz
  - SNR etc.: receiver-dependent
  - Ionosphere: full cycles
