## Task-1
### a) A receiver-satellite distance of 20190 km, on frequency L2 (1227.60 MHz) with a power of 30 W. Antenna gain: G = 12.1 dB
```
Pt = 30W = 14.7712 dBW
G = 12.1 dB
Pr = Pt + Gt + Gr - Loss
```

$$
L_f = \left( \frac{4\pi \cdot d \cdot f}{c} \right)^2 \Rightarrow
$$

$$
L_f[\text{dB}] = 20 \log_{10}(d) + 20 \log_{10}(f) + 20 \log_{10}\left(\frac{4\pi}{c}\right)
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
L_f[\text{dB}] = 20 \log_{10}(20190 \times 10^3 [\text{m}]) + 20 \log_{10}(1227.60 \times 10^6 [\text{Hz}]) - 147.55 = \\
146.1027 + 181.7811 - 147.55 = 180.3316 \text{ dB}
\end{aligned}
$$

`Pr = 14.7712 dBW + 12.1 dB - 180.3316 dB = -153.4604 dBW`
